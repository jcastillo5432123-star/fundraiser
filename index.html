Creative Youth
McAllen, Texas • Arts & Imagination

Creative Youth is bringing creativity to every kid in McAllen, Texas.

Founded by Joaquin Castillo, Creative Youth exists to build safe spaces where young people can paint, design, build, and tell their stories. We turn unused rooms into bright studios, partner with local venues, and create workshops that help youth feel seen and inspired.

Mission

How We Start

Launch a pop-up creative workshop in partnership with a local space like a coffee shop or community venue.

Why It Matters

Youth deserve access to art, mentorship, and safe spaces where they can imagine a future beyond their limits.
